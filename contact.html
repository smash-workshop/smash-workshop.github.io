Contact

 

Registration is required of all participants. Please email Baptiste Morisse with the following information:

  • Name, institution, career stage
  • Anticipated arrival/departure dates
  • Do you require financial support?
  • Would you like to contribute a talk? If so, please include a title and abstract

There is limited funding for students and early career researchers on a "first come first serve" basis. Please contact us if you require financial support.
